Ensuring Safety for All

The CBIT Transport Department prioritizes the safety of students and staff. We have implemented several measures to ensure a secure and comfortable travel experience.

General Safety Guidelines

  • All buses are regularly inspected and maintained.
  • Drivers undergo rigorous training and certification.
  • Speed limits are strictly enforced.
  • Emergency contact numbers are available in all buses.
  • Students must adhere to seating and standing protocols.

COVID-19 Precautions

  • Regular sanitization of buses.
  • Mandatory masks for all passengers and drivers.
  • Social distancing protocols inside the buses.
  • Availability of hand sanitizers at entry points.

Emergency Protocols

In case of emergencies, students and staff are advised to follow the instructions of the bus driver and transport coordinators. Emergency exits and fire extinguishers are available in all buses.
