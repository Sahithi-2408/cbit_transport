About Us

Welcome to CBIT Transport Department

The CBIT Transport Department is committed to providing safe, reliable, and efficient transportation services for students and staff. Our fleet of buses ensures timely connectivity to and from the college, covering various routes across the city.


Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure smooth and hassle-free transportation for everyone associated with CBIT. We strive to maintain the highest standards of safety and punctuality.


Our Services

  • Fixed routes covering major parts of the city
  • Timely schedules to ensure punctuality
  • Emergency helpline for transport-related queries
